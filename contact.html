Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete a task?

You can delete a task by clicking the trash icon on any task card, or use bulk delete in the Gallery view.

Are my tasks saved automatically?

Yes! All your tasks are automatically saved to your browser's local storage, so they'll be there when you return.

Can I use TaskFlow on mobile?

Absolutely! TaskFlow is fully responsive and works great on phones, tablets, and desktop computers.
